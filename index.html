Research Interest:

I am a 4th year Ph.D candidate at the Chinese University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Prof. Michael R. Lyu. My research concerns the text analysis, including neural machine translation and log analysis, which aims to provide a more reliability system for practical usage.

Our project LogPAI provides an end-to-end solution to intelligently manage and analyze logs for modern production systems using machine learning techniques. So far, the project has received 1500+ stars in total and the datasets Loghub have been downloaded for more than 8000 times by 370+ organizations. Feel free to contact us if you have any questions.
